Four Free Browser Tools I Am Reinforcing for Search

I am running a small AI programming outbound experiment around free browser tools.

The lesson this week is simple: shipping the site is not the finish line. Search only starts to care when the site stays healthy, gets submitted, receives public references, and keeps proving that it is maintained.

This round focuses on four weaker tools.

FreeTDEE
https://freetdee.com/

A free TDEE, BMR, calorie, and macro calculator. It is useful, but calorie keywords are competitive, so it needs more long-tail support and public references.

PupVax
https://pupvax.com/

A puppy vaccine schedule tracker. Dog owners search with very specific timing questions, so this site can grow through focused utility pages and consistent indexing.

ZonePlan
https://zoneplan.net/

A time zone meeting planner for remote teams. The category is evergreen and fragmented, which makes it a good long-tail SEO asset if maintained properly.

InvoicePad
https://invoicepad.net/

A free invoice generator for freelancers. I shortened the homepage title, checked the production domain again, and kept it inside the public maintenance trail.
