I am keeping a public maintenance trail for a small group of free browser tools built with AI.

This round is focused on four weaker sites that need more crawl signals and public references.

## 1. FreeTDEE

FreeTDEE is a browser-based calorie, BMR, TDEE, and macro calculator.

Link: https://freetdee.com/

Why I am reinforcing it:

- useful but still weak in search
- calculator intent is competitive
- needs more public references and long-tail coverage

## 2. PupVax

PupVax helps dog owners create a simple puppy vaccine schedule.

Link: https://pupvax.com/

Why it matters:

- new dog owners search with urgent intent
- the site has a clear utility page
- vaccine and reminder queries can become long-tail traffic

## 3. ZonePlan

ZonePlan is a meeting time zone planner for remote teams.

Link: https://zoneplan.net/

Why I am keeping it alive:

- remote team scheduling is evergreen
- the tool has strong daily utility
- time zone searches are very fragmented, which is good for long-tail SEO

## 4. InvoicePad

InvoicePad is a free invoice generator for freelancers.

Link: https://invoicepad.net/

Recent maintenance:

- homepage title was shortened
- production domain was checked again
- sitemap and robots are live
