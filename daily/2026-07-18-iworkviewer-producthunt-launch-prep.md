## What iWorkViewer does

iWorkViewer helps people open Apple iWork files on non-Apple devices.

It focuses on the first painful step: someone sends a `.pages`, `.numbers`, or `.key` file, and you are on Windows, Android, Linux, or a shared computer.

The goal is a lightweight browser workflow:

- no install
- no signup
- fast file preview
- useful for non-Apple users
