# $99 Small Tool Site SEO Audit

A fast, practical SEO/indexing audit for small browser tools, indie apps, calculators, SaaS side projects, and one-page utilities.

## Who this is for

This is for you if you have a live tool site but Google is barely sending traffic.

Good fit:

- free browser tools
- calculators
- converters
- invoice/template tools
- small SaaS landing pages
- Product Hunt launches

Not a fit:

## What I check

I review one site and send you a one-page action plan covering:

1. Indexing blockers
   - robots.txt
   - sitemap.xml
   - noindex
   - canonical
   - redirects
   - dead URLs

2. Search snippet leaks
   - homepage title
   - homepage description
   - top page titles
   - intent mismatch
   - CTR problems

3. Content gaps
   - missing how-to pages
   - missing comparison pages
   - thin tool pages
   - internal link gaps

4. Schema opportunities
   - FAQPage
   - WebApplication / SoftwareApplication
   - BreadcrumbList
   - Article / HowTo where useful

5. Backlink reality check
   - self-links vs real third-party links
   - nofollow / ugc traps
   - practical white-hat link targets

6. Deployment/indexing checklist
   - what to fix first
   - what to submit
   - what not to waste time on

## Deliverable

No 40-page agency PDF. Just what to fix.
